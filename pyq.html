How to use

Get the most out of these discussions.

These aren't just explanations — they're demonstrations of how to think about PSIR questions. Here's how to maximise your learning.

1

Attempt the question first

Pause the video, write your answer, then compare. The gap between your attempt and the model is where growth happens.

2

Focus on the approach, not just content

Notice how the question is deconstructed, how introductions are framed, and how conclusions tie back to the core argument.

3

Make micro-notes

Jot down unique angles, thinker references, and examples mentioned. These become your value-adds in the actual exam.

4

Re-attempt after 3 days

Spaced repetition solidifies learning. Write the same question again — you'll be surprised how much you retain.
